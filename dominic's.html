More About This Institute

St. Dominics Academy, Shikohabad is a Catholic, Christian, Minority Educational Institution imparting education to students irrespective of their sex, caste, creed, religion or social status to strengthen inter religious, inter communal and inter-cultural tie among the students so that they learn to respect all religions and cultures in our country and strive to achieve 'Unity in Diversity' as part of God's plan for humanity. It is an English medium co-educational school, according to the syllabus of Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi and is being run by St. Thomas Educational and Medical society, Etawah (Registered)
